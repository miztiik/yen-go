# Yen-Go

Yen-Go is an offline-first Go (Baduk/Weiqi) tsumego puzzle platform. Practice life-and-death problems at any skill level, anytime, anywhere — no account or internet connection required.

**[Play at yen-go.github.io](https://yen-go.github.io)**

---

## Game Modes

### Daily Puzzles

A new set of puzzles is published every day, one per difficulty level. Solve them to build your streak and track your progress over time.

### Puzzle Rush

A timed challenge mode. Solve as many puzzles as you can before the clock runs out. Results are tracked locally so you can improve on your personal best.

---

## Difficulty Levels

Yen-Go uses a nine-level system that maps to standard kyu and dan ranks:

| Level              | Rank Range | What to Expect              |
| ------------------ | ---------- | --------------------------- |
| Novice             | 30k – 26k  | Single-move problems        |
| Beginner           | 25k – 21k  | Basic life-and-death shapes |
| Elementary         | 20k – 16k  | Simple sequences            |
| Intermediate       | 15k – 11k  | Standard patterns           |
| Upper Intermediate | 10k – 6k   | Multi-step reading          |
| Advanced           | 5k – 1k    | Complex tesuji              |
| Low Dan            | 1d – 3d    | Dan-level reading           |
| High Dan           | 4d – 6d    | Professional patterns       |
| Expert             | 7d – 9d    | Master-level problems       |

## Controls

| Input       | Action                   |
| ----------- | ------------------------ |
| Click / Tap | Place a stone            |
| `H`         | Show next hint           |
| `S`         | Show full solution       |
| `R`         | Reset the puzzle         |
| `←` `→`     | Navigate between puzzles |

---

## Hints

When you are stuck, hints reveal information progressively so you can keep thinking rather than simply seeing the answer:

1. **Region** — Highlights the area of the board to focus on
2. **Technique** — Names the tesuji or concept involved (e.g., snapback, ladder)
3. **First Move** — Shows the correct opening move

---

## Progress and Achievements

All progress is stored locally in your browser. No account is needed and no data leaves your device.

- Solved puzzle history
- Daily streak tracking
- Over 30 achievements to unlock
- Personal statistics by level and technique

---

## Offline Play

Yen-Go is a Progressive Web App. After your first visit, the application and its puzzle data are cached locally and remain fully playable without an internet connection.
