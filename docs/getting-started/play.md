Welcome to Yen-Go, a fully offline Go (Baduk/Weiqi) tsumego puzzle platform!

## Quick Start

### Play Online

Visit **[yen-go.github.io](https://yen-go.github.io)** — no installation required!

### Play Offline

1. Download the app from [Releases](https://github.com/[org]/yen-go/releases)
2. Open `index.html` in your browser
3. Start solving puzzles!

## Game Modes

### Daily Puzzles

New puzzles every day across 9 difficulty levels:

- **Novice** (30k-26k) — Single-move problems
- **Beginner** (25k-21k) — Basic life/death shapes
- **Elementary** (20k-16k) — Simple sequences
- **Intermediate** (15k-11k) — Standard patterns
- **Upper Intermediate** (10k-6k) — Multi-step reading
- **Advanced** (5k-1k) — Complex tesuji
- **Low Dan** (1d-3d) — Dan-level reading
- **High Dan** (4d-6d) — Professional patterns
- **Expert** (7d-9d) — Master-level problems

### Puzzle Rush

Timed challenges to test your speed and accuracy. How many can you solve in 5 minutes?

## Controls

### Mouse/Touch

- **Click/Tap** an intersection to place a stone
- **Click/Tap** outside the board to pass (when allowed)

### Keyboard

| Key     | Action           |
| ------- | ---------------- |
| `←` `→` | Navigate puzzles |
| `H`     | Show hint        |
| `S`     | Show solution    |
| `R`     | Reset puzzle     |
| `Esc`   | Cancel           |

## Hints System

Three levels of progressive hints:

1. **Level 1** — Shows the region to focus on
2. **Level 2** — Reveals the technique type (e.g., "snapback")
3. **Level 3** — Shows the first move

## Progress

Your progress is saved locally in your browser:

- ✅ Solved puzzles
- 🔥 Daily streaks
- 🏆 Achievements (30+ badges)
- 📊 Statistics

_No account needed — your data stays on your device._

## Need Help?

- **Puzzle too hard?** Use hints (H key) or skip to the next one
- **Want to learn?** Review solutions after solving
- **Found a bug?** [Report it on GitHub](https://github.com/[org]/yen-go/issues)
